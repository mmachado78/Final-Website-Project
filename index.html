Home

Hello! My name is Marc Machado, and I am a first year student in the George Washington University School of Business. I created this website for Management Information Systems Technology (BADM 2301) with Professor Michael Rossetti. It is designed to provide a deeper look into me as a person, from my background to my passions. Using the items on the right side of the navigation bar at the top of the screen, you can read more about me, learn how I used my biggest passion to impact my community, check out my resume and find my contact information.

For this website, I created a repository in GitHub Desktop and wrote the HTML/CSS in Visual Studio Code. Using a template provided by Professor Rossetti, I added HTML and HTML5 semantic elements to produce a website that I hope is visually appealing and easy to navigate! I had no experience with these programs or HTML/CSS as a whole prior to arriving at GW, but with the help of Professor Rossetti, I now feel comfortable with it. I hope you like my website, and feel free to contact me with questions!
