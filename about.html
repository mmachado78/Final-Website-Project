About Me

I am from the small but growing city of Winchester, Virginia, which is about 60 miles northwest of Washington, D.C. I attended John Handley High School (which has been voted the most beautiful public high school in Virginia!), graduating with an advanced diploma in June of 2021. During high school, I was an active member of Kiwanis International, National Honors Society and Sociedad Honoraria Hispánica, among others.

On August 21, 2021, I began the next chapter of my life when I moved into GW eight days before the start of the fall semester. While I’ve had to persevere through some difficult moments that every first-year college student seems to experience, I’ve thus far enjoyed my time at the school and in the nation’s capital as a whole. My favorite class has been Introduction to Financial Accounting (ACCY 2001) with Professor Keehea Moon, for I love the puzzle-like aspect of recording journal entries.

Outside of school, I’ve enjoyed attending professional sporting events at Audi Field, Nationals Park and Capital One Arena, which is no surprise given my passion for sports, especially soccer. My love for the game stretches back for as long as I can remember; I began playing soccer at the age of five, eventually transitioning into coaching. In my free time, I watch whatever games are available to me. It is my goal to visit every Major League Soccer (MLS) stadium, and so far, I’ve been to quite a few. Below are four of my favorites. They are:

  1. Audi Field in Washington, D.C., home of D.C. United
  2. Mercedes-Benz Stadium in Atlanta, Georgia, home of Atlanta United FC
  3. TQL Stadium in Cincinatti, Ohio, home of FC Cincinnati
  4. Lower.com Field in Columbus, Ohio, home of the Columbus Crew
